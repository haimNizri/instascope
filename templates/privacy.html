InstaScope Connect — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

What data we collect

The InstaScope Connect Chrome extension accesses your Instagram session cookie (sessionid) when you explicitly click the "Connect Instagram" button. This is used solely to authenticate with Instagram's API on your behalf to provide analytics features.

How we use your data

  • Instagram session cookie: Sent securely to InstaScope servers to fetch your follower/following lists, post engagement data, and story viewers. We do not store your Instagram password.
  • Email address: Used for account login and subscription management only.
  • Instagram analytics data: Follower lists, engagement metrics, and analysis reports are stored in our database to provide the service.

What we do NOT do

  • We never access your Instagram password
  • We never post, like, comment, or take any actions on your Instagram account
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties
  • We never collect browsing history or activity outside of Instagram

Data storage

Your data is stored securely in our PostgreSQL database hosted on Render.com. Session cookies are encrypted at rest. You can request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us.

Permissions explained

  • cookies: Required to read your Instagram sessionid cookie (only from instagram.com) when you click Connect
  • activeTab: Required to detect when you're on instagram.com
  • storage: Used to save your login state locally in the extension

Data deletion

You can disconnect your Instagram account and delete all associated data at any time from your InstaScope account settings. To request complete account deletion, email us.
