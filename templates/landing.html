Frequently Asked Questions

Is my Instagram password safe? ▼

We never ask for or store your Instagram password. We use a session cookie (which you provide) for read-only access. We cannot post, like, comment, or take any action on your account.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Yes! Cancel anytime from your billing page. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No contracts, no commitments.

How does the AI work? ▼

We use Claude AI (by Anthropic) to analyze your account data and generate personalized insights, captions, and recommendations. Every generation is unique and tailored to your content niche.

What data do you collect? ▼

We only access your public profile data, follower/following lists, and post engagement metrics. We never sell your data or share it with third parties. See our privacy policy.

Does it work with private accounts? ▼

Yes! Since you connect your own session, we can access your own account data regardless of privacy settings. You can only analyze your own account (not others).
