Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your billing page anytime. You keep access until the end of your billing period.

How does the AI work?

We use Claude AI by Anthropic to analyze your data and generate personalized insights, captions, and recommendations. Every generation is unique.

What data do you collect?

Only your public profile data, follower lists, and post engagement. We never sell your data. See our privacy policy.

Yes. Since you connect your own session, we can access your data regardless of privacy settings. You can only analyze your own account.

How to See Who Unfollowed You on Instagram

Instagram doesn't notify you when someone unfollows you. IGAI solves this by taking snapshots of your follower list and comparing them over time. Every time you scan, we detect exactly who unfollowed you — with details like their gender, account type, and when they left.

Unlike other tools, IGAI uses AI to analyze patterns — connecting unfollower events to your content, helping you understand why people leave and how to keep them.

What Are Ghost Followers on Instagram?

Ghost followers are accounts that follow you but never engage — they don't like, comment, or view your stories. They hurt your engagement rate, which affects how Instagram's algorithm shows your content.

IGAI identifies ghost followers by cross-referencing your follower list with your post engagement data. We also find "secret fans" — people who regularly engage with your content but don't follow you yet.

Instagram Follower Analytics for Creators

Understanding your audience is key to growing on Instagram. IGAI provides AI-powered demographics analysis — showing where your followers are from, what languages they speak, and their gender distribution.

Our Content Advisor analyzes your posts to find the best times to post, which hashtags drive the most engagement, and what content types work best for your niche.

Who Doesn't Follow Me Back on Instagram?

Ever wondered who you follow that doesn't follow you back? IGAI's Relationships feature shows you instantly — with filters by gender and a complete list of mutual follows, one-way followers, and accounts that don't reciprocate.

Clean up your following list, find your biggest fans, and understand your follower relationships at a glance.
