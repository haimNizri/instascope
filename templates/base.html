Instagram session needed for follower tracking
How?

1. Open instagram.com in Chrome (make sure you're logged in)

2. Press F12 (or Cmd+Option+I)

3. Go to Application tab → Cookies → https://www.instagram.com

4. Find sessionid and copy its Value

5. Paste it above and click Connect. Done! (one-time setup)
